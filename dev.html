Common Questions

FAQ

Do you service both Tauranga and Mount Maunganui?+

Yes — we cover Tauranga, Mount Maunganui, Papamoa, and the wider Bay of Plenty. We come directly to your home, driveway, or workplace. Just let us know your location when booking.

How long does a full car detail take?+

An Express Wash takes around 1 hour. A Full Detail typically takes 2–3 hours depending on vehicle size. A Premium Detail with paint correction can take 4–6 hours. We'll give you a time estimate when booking.

Do I need to provide water or power?+

For most jobs, access to a garden tap and a standard power outlet is helpful. If you're at a workplace or location without access, we can bring our own water tank and generator for a small additional fee.

What's the difference between Cut & Polish and Ceramic Coating?+

Cut & Polish is a paint correction process — it removes scratches, swirl marks and oxidation to restore your paint's original clarity. Ceramic Coating is a protective layer applied after correction that bonds to the paint and lasts years. They're often done together for the best result.

How do I book a car detail in Tauranga?+

Fill in the booking form on this page, or give us a call or text. We'll confirm your time, location, and chosen package within 24 hours.
