The Process

What's Included in Every Cut & Polish

Our cut and polish is a multi-stage paint correction performed by hand and machine. Every vehicle is assessed individually — we match the right compounds and pads to your specific paint condition.

We require the vehicle for a minimum of 2 days to ensure we never rush the process. Quality over speed, every time.

  • Full vehicle wash & decontamination
  • Clay bar treatment to remove surface contaminants
  • Paint thickness measurement before correction
  • Machine compound cut — scratch & swirl removal
  • Medium polish to refine and clarify paint
  • Fine finishing polish for maximum gloss
  • Panel wipe-down & inspection under lighting
  • Finishing wax or sealant applied for protection
  • Final detail & quality check
Why Cut & Polish

The Benefits

More than a clean — a full restoration of your paint's clarity and depth.

01
Removes Scratches & Swirls

Light to medium scratches, swirl marks and buffer trails are removed through controlled paint correction — not covered up.

02
Restores Faded Paint

UV exposure and oxidation cause paint to look dull and chalky. A cut and polish brings back depth, gloss and the original colour vibrancy.

03
Increases Resale Value

A professionally corrected finish makes your vehicle look newer and more cared for — directly impacting its perceived value and saleability.

04
Prepares for Coating

If you're considering ceramic coating, a cut and polish first ensures the coating bonds to flawless paint — maximising protection and longevity.

05
Long-Lasting Results

With proper maintenance after correction, results last significantly longer than a standard polish — especially when paired with a sealant.

06
Expert Assessment

Every job starts with a thorough paint inspection. We only correct what needs correcting — protecting your paint thickness and long-term integrity.
