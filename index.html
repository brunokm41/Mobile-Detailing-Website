Common Questions

FAQ

Do you service both Tauranga and Mount Maunganui? +

Yes — we cover Tauranga, Mount Maunganui, Papamoa, and the wider Bay of Plenty. We come directly to your home, driveway, or workplace. Just let us know your location when booking.

How long does a full car detail take in Tauranga? +

An Express Wash takes around 1 hour. A Full Detail typically takes 2–3 hours depending on vehicle size and condition. A Premium Detail with paint correction can take 4–6 hours. We'll give you a time estimate when booking.

Do I need to provide water or power? +

For most jobs, access to a garden tap and a standard power outlet is helpful. If you're at a workplace or location without access, let us know and we can bring our own water tank and generator for a small additional fee.

How do I book a car detail in Mount Maunganui or Papamoa? +

Simply fill in the booking form on this page, or give us a call or text. We'll confirm your time, location, and chosen package within 24 hours. We service Mount Maunganui and Papamoa regularly throughout the week.
